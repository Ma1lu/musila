Design for Social Cause

Design with Photography

I sought to come up with a series of posters to be used to highlight corruption and the misuse of democratic power, particularly aimed at the East African population following high profile cases in Kenya and Tanzania. The aim was to have an indirect sarcastic tone, allowing the viewers to come to deduce some of the messaging for themselves. My method here was to start with that end message, a tagline to base the content around. This involved research on the topic beforehand, creating objectivity and highlighting key messages and issues.
